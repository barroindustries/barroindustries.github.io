# Brilliant Steel — Partner Portal Guide
## Barro Industries Operations System

**Version 1.0 · June 2026**

---

## Welcome, Brilliant Steel Team

This guide explains how to use the Barro Industries Partner Portal. The portal allows Brilliant Steel representatives to build and submit quotations, manage client data, access shared files, and track project status — all in one place.

You are accessing this system as a **partner**, not as an employee of Barro Industries. Your portal shows only the sections relevant to your work with us.

---

## 1. Getting Access

Before your first login, a Barro Industries administrator will:
- Create your partner account with your Brilliant Steel email address
- Send you a temporary password

If you have not received login credentials, contact your Barro Industries project coordinator.

---

## 2. How to Log In

1. Open the app URL in your browser or phone.
   > Tip: On mobile, tap the browser's share button → **"Add to Home Screen"** for easy access.
2. On the login screen, tap **"Login as Partner"**.
3. Enter your **Brilliant Steel email address** and **password**, then tap **Sign In**.
4. First time? Tap **Forgot Password** to set your password via your email inbox.

**Important:** Always select **"Login as Partner"** on the login screen. Selecting Employee or Admin will show the wrong dashboard.

---

## 3. Your Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the Partner Dashboard showing:
- **Active Quotations** — quotes currently in progress or submitted
- **Pending Approvals** — quotes awaiting review from Barro Industries
- **Recent Activity** — latest updates on quotes and files
- **Quick Links** — shortcuts to Quote Builder, Quotations summary, and Files

---

## 4. Quote Builder

The Quote Builder is the core tool for creating cost estimates and project quotations.

### Creating a New Quote

1. Go to **Quote Builder** in the sidebar or bottom navigation.
2. Fill in the project details:
   - **Client Name** — the end client or project name
   - **Project Description** — brief description of the scope
   - **Quote Date** — defaults to today; adjust if needed
3. Add **line items** by tapping **"+ Add Item"**:
   - Item Name / Description
   - Quantity
   - Unit Price
   - Any applicable notes
4. The system auto-calculates:
   - Subtotal per line
   - Overall total
   - VAT / tax if applicable
5. Review the summary at the bottom.
6. Tap **"Save Draft"** to save without submitting, or **"Submit Quote"** to send it to Barro Industries for review.

### Editing a Draft

Open the quote from the **Quotations** page, make changes, and tap **Save Draft** again. You can edit drafts at any time before submission.

### After Submission

Once submitted, the quote status changes to **"Pending Review."** A Barro Industries manager will review and either:
- **Approve** — you will see the status change to Approved
- **Request Revision** — you will see a note with feedback; edit and resubmit
- **Reject** — with a reason provided

---

## 5. Quotations Summary

Go to **Quotations** in the sidebar to see all your submitted and draft quotes.

The table shows:
- Quote reference number
- Client name
- Date submitted
- Total amount
- Current status (Draft / Pending / Approved / Rejected)

Tap any quote to open and view the full details or download a copy.

---

## 6. Client Data

The **Client Data** section stores information about the clients relevant to your projects with Barro Industries.

You can:
- View existing client profiles and contact details
- Add new clients when starting a new project
- Update client information as needed

When creating a quote, client data auto-fills if the client already exists in the system.

---

## 7. Files

The **Files** section is a shared document space between Brilliant Steel and Barro Industries.

### Uploading Files

1. Go to **Files** in the sidebar or bottom navigation.
2. Tap the upload area or drag and drop a file.
3. Files are automatically saved to the company's central storage system and organized under the **Brilliant Steel** folder.
4. Once uploaded, a shareable link is created. Share this link with your Barro Industries contact if needed.

### What to Upload

Use this section for:
- Material specifications and datasheets
- Technical drawings and CAD files
- Revised quotation documents
- Delivery receipts or purchase orders
- Any documents requested by Barro Industries

### Accessing Shared Files

Files shared by Barro Industries with your account will appear here. Tap the file name or link to open or download.

---

## 8. Notifications

The system sends updates for the following events:
- A quote you submitted has been approved or rejected
- A quote has a revision request with notes
- A file has been shared with you
- A message or note has been added to your account

Check the **notification bell** in the top bar for alerts. Notifications are also visible in the sidebar badge count.

## 9. Common Questions

**Q: I forgot my password.**
A: On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**, enter your Brilliant Steel email, and follow the reset link in your inbox.

**Q: I submitted a quote but cannot find it.**
A: Go to **Quotations** in the sidebar. All submitted quotes are listed there sorted by date.

**Q: Can I edit a quote after submission?**
A: Once submitted, quotes cannot be edited unless Barro Industries sends it back for revision. To make changes before a decision, contact your Barro Industries coordinator to request it be returned to Draft.

**Q: A file I uploaded is not showing.**
A: Files may take a few seconds to appear after upload. Refresh the Files page. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or try a different file format.

**Q: My quote shows "Rejected" — what now?**
A: Open the quote to read the rejection reason. Create a new quote with the requested changes and resubmit.

**Q: Who do I contact for access issues?**
A: Contact your Barro Industries project coordinator for login help or account issues.

---

## 10. Tips for Working Efficiently

- **Save drafts often** while building complex quotes. You can always return and complete them later.
- **Use clear item descriptions** in quotes so the Barro Industries team can review and approve faster.
- **Upload supporting files** (specs, drawings) along with your quote to reduce back-and-forth.
- **Check notifications regularly** — approvals and revision requests appear there first.
- **Add to home screen** on your phone so the portal is always one tap away.

# Barro Industries — Employee Guide
## Operations System — How to Use the App

**Version 1.0 · June 2026**

---

## Welcome

This guide covers everything you need to use the Barro Industries Operations System. Bookmark it or keep it saved on your phone. If something is unclear, ask your manager or check the Help page inside the app.

---

## 1. Getting Access

Before your first login, your manager will:
- Create your employee account with your work email address
- Assign your department and role
- Send you a temporary password

If you have not received your login credentials, contact your manager directly.

---

## 2. How to Log In

1. Open the app URL in your phone or desktop browser.
   > Tip: On your phone, tap the share button → **"Add to Home Screen"** so it works like a regular app.
2. On the login screen, tap **"Login as Employee"**.
3. Enter your **work email** and **password**, then tap **Sign In**.
4. If it is your first time, tap **Forgot Password** to set a new password via your email inbox.

**Important:** Always log in as **Employee**, not Admin or Partner. Using the wrong login type will show you the wrong dashboard.

---

## 3. Your Dashboard

When you log in, you land on your personal dashboard. Here you will see:

- **KPI Score** — your current performance score for the month, shown as a percentage and a progress bar.
- **Overdue Tasks** — a red banner appears if any of your tasks are past their due date. Address these first.
- **Task Summary** — how many tasks are assigned, in progress, and completed this month.
- **Attendance Summary** — your logged attendance days for the current month.

---

## 4. Logging Your Attendance (Do This Every Day)

Attendance is tracked daily and affects your KPI score. Do not skip this.

1. In the bottom navigation, tap **Home** (or use the sidebar → **My Dashboard**).
2. Find the **Attendance** section on your dashboard.
3. Tap **Log Full Day** if you worked a complete shift, or **Log Half Day** if you worked a partial shift.
4. You can only log once per day. The system records the current date automatically.

> **Why this matters:** Your attendance score is part of your monthly KPI calculation. Missing days without logging will lower your score.

---

## 5. Your Tasks

### Viewing Tasks

Go to **My Tasks** in the sidebar or bottom navigation. You will see:
- All tasks assigned to you
- Status: To Do / In Progress / Done
- Priority: High (red dot) / Medium (yellow) / Low (grey)
- Due date for each task

### Updating a Task

1. Tap the task to open it.
2. Change the **Status** using the dropdown (To Do → In Progress → Done).
3. Add a **comment or update** if required by your manager.
4. Tap **Save**.

Always update your tasks as you work on them. Your president and manager check this in real time.

### Overdue Tasks

If a task is past its due date and not marked Done, it shows as overdue (red). These affect your KPI. Mark tasks done as soon as they are completed, even if slightly late.

---

## 6. KPI Score — How It Is Calculated

Your KPI score is shown on your dashboard each month. It is calculated as:

```
KPI Score = (Task Score × 70%) + (Deliverable Score × 30%)
```

**Task Score (70%)** — based on how many of your assigned tasks you complete during the month.

**Deliverable Score (30%)** — set manually by the president based on the quality of your work, deliverables submitted, and overall contribution. This is updated by management.

**Attendance** is tracked separately and shown alongside your KPI.

Your KPI score affects your final monthly pay calculation.

---

## 7. Personal Finance

Go to **Personal Finance** in the sidebar. Here you can see:

### Your Salary Breakdown
- **Base Salary** — your fixed monthly salary
- **Allowances** — any additional allowances
- **Deductions** — SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, tax, and other deductions
- **Net Pay** — base + allowances − deductions
- **KPI Bonus/Adjustment** — additional amount tied to your KPI score
- **Final Pay** — your actual take-home amount for the month

### Salary History
A table showing the past 12 months of your payroll records including KPI score per month.

### Year-to-Date (YTD)
Your total final pay earned so far this calendar year, shown at the top of the salary section.

---

## 8. Cash Advance Requests

You can request a cash advance through the app.

1. Go to **Personal Finance** → scroll to the **Cash Advance** section.
2. Fill in:
   - **Amount** — how much you need
   - **Date Needed** — when you need the cash
   - **Repayment Date** — when you will pay it back (usually next salary)
   - **Reason** — brief explanation
3. Tap **Submit Request**.
4. Your manager or president will approve or reject the request. You will see the status updated in your Cash Advance table.

**Note:** Cash advances must be approved before any disbursement. Do not assume approval until the status shows "Approved."

---

## 9. Files

Go to **Files** in the sidebar or bottom navigation to upload and access documents.

- Tap the upload area or drag a file to attach it.
- Files are automatically saved to the company's central storage (Google Drive or Cloud Storage).
- Once uploaded, a link is created — tap it to open the file anytime.
- Files are organized by your department automatically.

Use this section to submit work deliverables, reports, or any documents your manager requests.

---

## 10. Company & Department Pages

The **Company** section shows:
- Company announcements and updates
- Department information and team contacts

Your **department tab** (e.g. Admin, Finance, Marketing) shows department-specific content relevant to your team.

---

## 11. Profile & Settings

Tap your avatar or name at the top of the sidebar to access your profile. You can:
- Update your display name
- Change your profile photo
- Change your password

---

## 12. Help Inside the App

There is a **Help / Guide** section in the sidebar. Tap it anytime for:
- Step-by-step instructions for every feature
- A storage setup guide (for admin reference)
- Quick tips for common tasks

## Common Questions

**Q: I forgot my password.**
A: On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**, enter your email, and check your inbox for a reset link.

**Q: I logged attendance but it is not showing.**
A: Attendance may take a few seconds to refresh. Pull the screen down to refresh or navigate away and back.

**Q: My KPI score seems low.**
A: Check if you have overdue tasks (mark them done if completed) and confirm attendance is logged for all working days. The deliverable score (30%) is set by management.

**Q: I submitted a Cash Advance but see no status change.**
A: Requests are reviewed by management. Expect a response within 1 business day.

**Q: Files are not uploading.**
A: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact your admin.
